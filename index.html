Compare real players using real season stats.

This version focuses on raw statistics for football nerds like myself, instead of fake scouting language through pulling player information and 2025 regular season stats from ESPN's public JSON endpoints. Choose any two players, and it'll highlight the better number in green.

Comparison rule

Green means the player with the higher value wins that stat. If both values are the same, neither side is highlighted. NOTE: QB's sacks are counted as "Sacks Recieved", I left the logic still as having more sacks to be better, because for a QB, more sacks should act as a greater obstacle and is typically not a fault of the QB.
